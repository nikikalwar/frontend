Chicken curry Receipe

BY NIKI, On Oct 5,jump to Receipe

Chicken curry for beginners. This is a detailed guide on cooking chicken curry/masala at home. Well, always wanted to cook chicken but had trouble with making it tasty so worry no more.
Had trouble making it delicious but this guide will take your cooking skills to a differnt level.



I have grown up cooking various food items but chicken is my favourite one. So ,making a chicken curry I learnt from my roommate.

Steps for Cooking!

  1. Heat some oil around 50ml or more depending upon the chicken in heavy buttom pan.
  2. Add some jeera to the oil and let the jeera get darker.
  3. Cut some green chillies and add to the pan.
  4. Wash the chicken and keep it in the pan and leave it for fry .
  5. Once the chicken starts looking a little red add oninons into the pan.
  6. Fry for sometime,add some garlic paste leave it for sometime
  7. Now add the chicken masala, garam masala,haldi,chilli, coriander powder
  8. leave it to fry for sometime.
  9. Now add tomatoes, coriander leaves and leave it for sometime.
  10. Add water according to the requirement of Gravy.
  11. Enjoy!!

Wrapping up

  • Check the salt if its fine
  • check the gravy if it is good
  • Now you are a better cook
